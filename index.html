Head Start Program

A structured university preparation program designed to help high school students transition confidently into higher education through real university courses, academic workshops, and supervised campus experience.

Apply Now

Courses Offered

Business Finance

Introduces financial literacy, core business concepts, and economic thinking essential for students interested in business and economics majors.

ESL (English as a Second Language)

Strengthens academic English skills required for university coursework, writing assignments, presentations, and exams.

Introduction to Data Science

Builds logical thinking and introduces data analysis concepts increasingly required in modern university programs.

Online Enrollment Exam & Scholarship

All applicants will take an online enrollment exam that evaluates English and Mathematics skills to ensure readiness for university-level study.

Students who pass the exam will receive a 50% scholarship for English and Math-related courses. Scholarships are awarded purely on academic merit.
